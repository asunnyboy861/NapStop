# Pricing Configuration

## Monetization Model: Paid Download

- **Price**: $2.99 (One-time purchase)
- **IAP**: Not required
- **Subscription**: None

## App Store Connect Pricing
- **Price Tier**: Tier 3 = $2.99

## Rationale
- Guide specifies $1.99-$4.99 range for commuters
- $2.99 is competitive vs DistAlarm (free+IAP) and WakePoint (subscription)
- One-time purchase eliminates subscription fatigue (key differentiator)
- No ongoing server costs (100% local data) justifies one-time pricing
- Premium positioning vs free+ad competitors
